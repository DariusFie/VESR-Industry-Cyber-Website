Healthcare Industry

Industry Overview

The healthcare industry focuses on providing medical services, manufacturing medical equipment, and developing pharmaceuticals. It plays a vital role in maintaining public health and advancing medical research.

My Role

I work as a Health Information Technician, responsible for managing patient records, ensuring data accuracy, and supporting clinical workflows through electronic health systems.

Ethical Issue

One ethical issue in my role is maintaining patient confidentiality. Mishandling sensitive health data can lead to privacy violations and loss of trust.

Cybersecurity Issues

Two major cybersecurity threats in healthcare include ransomware attacks on hospital networks and phishing attempts targeting staff to gain access to patient data.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Best practices include using multi-factor authentication, encrypting patient records, conducting regular security audits, and training staff on recognizing cyber threats.
