My Personal Values

Honesty

Honesty is very important to me because not only does it build trust, but it also creates clarity and reflects your beliefs and values. It shows courage and accountability; when you're honest with yourself and the people around you, it can take you very far in life. All in all, it represents growth and strengthens connections.

I developed this value because I myself have those moments when I'm not being honest with myself and denying things around me, knowing deep down I should be giving myself those hard truths, because it builds character and enhances my mental state.

Compassion

Compassion is a value to me because showing empathy and kindness to others is essential. The goal is not only to always treat people the way I want to be treated but also to understand and support people no matter who they are or how they look. And with that, compassion comes into play tremendously.

I developed this value because in this day and age, lots of negativity goes on in the world, so showing compassion no matter the situation can bring light to a person or people in general. It's all about being kind and seeing things in a better light.

Growth

Growth is one of my biggest and most important values because I value learning, evolving, and pushing myself to be better every day. Whether it's learning from school or learning more about myself and how I am, it's all important. Not only that, but growth can also give you a boost in mood and energy.

I developed this value because, being a college student, I know that personal development is a lifelong journey. When aging and growing in general, growth will always be needed, especially when it comes to knowing yourself and character development.
